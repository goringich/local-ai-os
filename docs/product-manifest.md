## Operating contract

- ICP: One technical owner of a private Linux workstation with a repeated, expensive context, recovery, or local-AI workflow.
- Problem: The owner loses verified project context or recovery confidence across restarts and cannot safely turn private local-AI tooling into a measured repeatable workflow.
- Outcome: A bounded documented local-first workflow with a visible result, acceptance report, and recoverable operating boundary.
- Wedge: One measured diagnostic for one repeated context or recovery workflow; not a sale of the entire stack.
- First visible result: A scoped diagnostic baseline and a sanitized before/after or acceptance artifact.
- Paid acquisition: forbidden
