# Roadmap

The roadmap is intentionally bounded by evidence rather than a promise of a universal AI operating system.

## Next evidence

The next commercial milestone is an owner-approved external workflow with baseline criteria agreed before work, a sanitized proof artifact, and explicit permission to share it.

- More founder proofs only when their evidence is public-safe.
- Deployment automation only after its topology and rollback contract are proven.
- Broader integrations only when they preserve the existing control boundaries.
