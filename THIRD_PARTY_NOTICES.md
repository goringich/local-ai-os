## Not cleared for automatic redistribution

- wallpaper collections;
- icon and cursor packs;
- GTK, SDDM, Kitty and Rofi themes copied from separate projects;
- fonts;
- third-party scripts without an adjacent license or notice.

LOCAL AI OS may be sold as integration, diagnostic, support and recovery work while these assets remain excluded from a distributable image. Complete the asset-level source/license inventory before publishing an installer or image that conveys them.
